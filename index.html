Welcome to Cosmic Guard

Welcome to Cosmic Guard, an epic space adventure where your journey begins not with a laser blaster, but with knowledge! Before you embark on thrilling space missions, explore distant galaxies, or mine rare resources on asteroids, you'll need to master the fundamentals of cosmic physics.

Your first stop is the University of the Stars, where you'll train with the brightest minds to understand the forces that shape the universe. From gravitational assists to orbital mechanics, you’ll gain the skills needed to navigate the stars. Once you’ve completed your training, you’ll be ready to join the Starfleet, build your own fleet, and conquer the challenges of space exploration.

Prepare to explore, learn, and become a cosmic legend!
